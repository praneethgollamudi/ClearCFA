Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

ClearCFA ("we", "us") is a CFA exam preparation tool. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it's handled.

What we collect

  • Email address — used to identify your account and communicate with you about your subscription.
  • Quiz session data — topics studied, scores, time taken, and answers. Used to personalise your practice sessions and calculate your pass probability.
  • Spaced repetition deck — your flashcard states and review history. Stored to enable the memory system.
  • Usage statistics — daily question counts and feature usage. Used to enforce free tier limits.
  • Payment reference — if you upgrade to Pro, we store confirmation that payment was verified. We do not store card numbers or UPI PINs — payments are made directly via your UPI app.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, phone number, or address unless you provide them voluntarily.
  • We do not use advertising trackers or sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not store your UPI PIN, bank account details, or payment credentials.

How data is stored

Your data is stored in two places:

  • Your browser's localStorage — quiz history, flashcard deck, and preferences are stored locally on your device for fast offline access.
  • Supabase — a cloud database hosted on AWS (eu-west region) where we back up your progress so it can be restored if you switch devices.

AI-generated content

Questions are generated using Anthropic's Claude API. Your quiz session context (topic, difficulty, weak areas) is sent to Claude to generate personalised questions. No personally identifiable information is sent — only study performance data. Anthropic's data processing is governed by their privacy policy.

Cookies

We do not use advertising cookies. The app uses localStorage (not cookies) to persist your session and preferences.

Your rights

  • Access — you can export your data from the app's Settings screen at any time.
  • Deletion — email gspbuilds@gmail.com to request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Correction — contact us to correct any inaccurate information.

Children

ClearCFA is intended for adults preparing for the CFA examination. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be communicated via the app. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when changes were last made.
